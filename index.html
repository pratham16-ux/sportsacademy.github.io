How training is structured

The Stackly method

Stage 1

Assessment week

Every new athlete is benchmarked on speed, mobility and sport-specific skill before being placed in a batch — never by age alone.

Stage 2

Foundation block

Eight weeks on movement fundamentals and conditioning, run alongside light sport-specific drilling.

Stage 3

Skill progression

Technical coaching intensifies; athletes start being rotated into scrimmage and match-play settings.

Stage 4

Competition exposure

Selected athletes are entered into district and state-level meets, with a coach attending every fixture.
