Before you enrol

How a free trial works

Every Saturday morning

Trial sessions run 7:00 – 9:00am at all three campuses. No advance payment, no equipment needed — just show up.

One-on-one with the coach

After the session, every child gets ten minutes with the batch coach. Parents are welcome to sit in and ask anything.

Assessment result the same day

Our coaches share a batch recommendation and a suggested start date before you leave. No waiting for a callback.

Prefer a quick message?

Reach the front desk on WhatsApp — we're online 6am – 8pm, every day.
