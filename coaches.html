Why our coaches are different

What it takes to coach here

Competed at state level or higher

No exceptions — every coach has played competitively, not just studied the sport.

Holds a recognised coaching certification

Verified against the relevant federation before any offer is made.

Passes a child-safety background check

Mandatory for every coach, renewed annually.

Completes a paid trial term

New coaches shadow a senior coach for one full term before leading a batch solo.
